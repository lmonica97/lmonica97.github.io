About Me

I'm a software engineer who is passionate to learning new ways to improve our society. Before coding, I was a Dental Assistant pursuing a Dental Hygiene degree. I decided the path wasn't meant for me due the fact that I always felt like everyday was the same. There was nothing more I can learn, and it became dull. I knew I wanted to be in a field where I can always encounter new problems and have to innovate to find ways to solve them.

I joined App Academy after doing some research for bootcamps. After applying more than 1000 hours in four months in the bootcamp, I was able to learn many technologies such as Javascript, React, Redux, AWS, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Mongoose, and many more. With these technologies, I was able to apply my knowledge and create my own projects. You can check them out here:

Besides coding, you can catch me either at the gym during my free time, or hanging out with my cat, Miko. I am also an avid Mechanical Keyboard enthusiast.
